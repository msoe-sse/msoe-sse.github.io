What are Lightning Talks?

Lightning talks are quick 5-15 minute presentations that explore a certain topic in the domain of software engineering. They are delivered during a SSE General Meeting and can be about anything you want! For example, you could give a talk on a new web framework, or maybe a new programming paradigm you just learned about. The goal is to share information and encourage collaboration in new technologies. Check out the archives below to see what members have previously presented!
